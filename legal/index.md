## Privacy Policy

### Data We Collect  
- **Location Data**: Collected for core app functionality, such as verifying when users are together. Only coarse location is used. Detailed location is not stored.  
- **Photos**: Collected when users upload or share photos. Stored securely.  
- **User Identifiers**: Includes usernames, email addresses or account IDs.  
- **Social Connections**: Data about friends or social graph for app functionality.  
- **Logs and Analytics**: Basic log data and usage analytics.  

### Why We Collect Data  
- **Core App Functionality**: To enable features like location-based hangouts, content sharing, and account management.  
- **Safety and Performance**: To detect abuse, maintain service reliability and improve the app experience.  

### How Data Is Stored  
- Data is stored on secure servers and may be processed by service providers such as Supabase.  
- We do not share personal data with third parties except as required to operate the service.  

### How Data Is Shared  
- Data is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising.  
- Service providers under contract may process data to provide infrastructure and analytics.  

### Data Deletion  
- Users may request account deletion within the app. When an account is deleted, user-generated content and personal data are removed from our servers within a reasonable time.  
- Logs may persist for a short period for security and auditing purposes.
